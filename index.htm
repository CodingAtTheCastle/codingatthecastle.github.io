What is Castle Coding?

Castle Coding is an open coding event at The Castle (the coolest and most welcoming co-working space in Stockholm).

It is free for anyone to join, both members of The Castle and outsiders are welcome!

The format is "self study". Maybe you want to learn something new, start a fun project or just hang out? Castle Coding is the place for you!
