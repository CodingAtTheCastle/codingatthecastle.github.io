Welcome to Castle Coding

Every wednesday it is "Castle Coding" at The Castle.

The event is for everyone that :

  • Is curious about learning what code is!
  • Taking your current skills to the next level!
  • Doing your own fun project!

The format is "self study" but dont be afraid to ask for some tips and tricks from someone who knows just a little bit more than you.

Location: Spegelsalen (Quiet room)

All levels are welcome! There will be wine!
